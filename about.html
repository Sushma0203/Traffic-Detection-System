About Us

Welcome to Vision, where innovation meets passion! We are a team of dedicated professionals committed to delivering exceptional services to our valued customers. Our journey began in 2024, with a vision to save lives through the effective routing of emergency vehicles.

Our Story

Founded by Sushma Thapa along with investor Sulav Shakya, Vision was born out of a desire to treasure lives. Over the years, we have grown from a small startup to a state-level entity, thanks to the trust and support of our customers.

Our Mission

At Vision, our mission is to embrace every life. We believe in core values such as quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction, and we strive to incorporate these principles into everything we do.

What We Do

We specialize in locating emergency vehicles and finding the best routes to maintain and prevent heavy traffic. Our team is passionate about the work, and we work tirelessly to innovate and improve, ensuring that our offerings meet the highest standards.
